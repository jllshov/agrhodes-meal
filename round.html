🧹 Cleaning Instructions
☝️Clean from clean to dirty, working from top to bottom.
1. Assemble supplies. Gather the disinfectant and cleaning equipment needed.
2. Wear appropriate PPE before beginning.
3. Clean and disinfect surfaces using a cloth rag or sponge immersed in germicidal solution (change rags/gloves as needed):
  • Bed and mattress (Terminal Clean)
  • High-touch surfaces: bed rails/controls, light switch, door knobs, IV/G-tube pole grab area, call box/button, remotes, bedside/tray table, chairs
  • Over-sink light (Terminal Clean)
  • Bathroom: sink, light switch, doorknobs, handrails by toilet, toilet seat/flush handle/bedpan cleaner
4. Clean the floor. Sweep, remove litter, dust, then mop using a flat mop immersed in germicidal detergent.
5. Privacy curtain. Inspect, clean, or replace as needed — always during terminal cleaning.
6. Change rags/gloves and perform hand hygiene.
7. Check & restock. Trash cans clean before new liner; PPE, sanitizer, soap, paper products stocked.
8. Mop the restroom floor using a flat mop immersed in germicidal detergent.
9. Clean your equipment. Wipe down mop handle, bucket, and containers with germicidal solution.
10. Remove gloves and perform hand hygiene.
⚠ Special Precautions / Isolation Rooms

A. Use the PPE indicated on the precaution sign before entering; remove before leaving.

B. Verify the disinfectant kills the isolation organism — know its contact time.

C. Don't dust mop or dry-dust furniture — use a mop/cloth immersed in disinfectant.
